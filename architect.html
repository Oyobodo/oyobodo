Architect

RealEstateCo is seeking a creative and experienced Architect to join our innovative team. You'll be instrumental in designing functional, safe, and visually appealing structures for our real estate developments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design building projects from concept to completion
  • Create detailed blueprints, drawings, and models
  • Collaborate with engineers, contractors, and clients
  • Ensure compliance with local building codes and regulations
  • Present designs to stakeholders and incorporate feedback

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Architecture
  • 3+ years of experience in architectural design
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, or similar tools
  • Strong visual and spatial awareness
  • Architectural license preferred

Location: Hybrid – Los Angeles, CA
