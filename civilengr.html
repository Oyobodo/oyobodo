Civil Engineer

We are looking for a skilled and detail-oriented Civil Engineer to join our team. You'll be responsible for designing, planning, and overseeing construction and maintenance of building structures and infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, develop and manage infrastructure projects
  • Conduct on-site investigations and analyze data
  • Assess potential risks, materials, and costs
  • Ensure compliance with safety and environmental regulations
  • Collaborate with architects, contractors, and other engineers

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering or related field
  • 2+ years of experience in civil engineering projects
  • Strong knowledge of AutoCAD, Civil 3D, or similar software
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
  • Professional Engineer (PE) license preferred

Location: On-site – Dallas, TX
