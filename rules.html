Company Rules & Guidelines

  1. All real estate agents must be duly licensed and in good standing with local regulatory authorities.
  2. All property listings must be verified for authenticity and accuracy before publication.
  3. Agents are required to maintain professionalism in all client interactions and communications.
  4. Client information must be kept confidential and handled in compliance with data protection laws.
  5. All marketing materials and advertisements must reflect truthful and non-deceptive content.
  6. Agents must adhere to company code of ethics and conflict of interest policies at all times.
  7. Punctuality and reliability in attending property viewings and meetings are mandatory.
  8. Agents must report any legal disputes or regulatory issues to management promptly.
  9. Misuse of company resources or branding without approval is strictly prohibited.
  10. Violation of these rules may result in disciplinary action, including termination of affiliation.
